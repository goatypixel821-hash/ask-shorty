# Quality Check: Qwen vs Haiku

For each test video check:

## Shorty Quality
- [ ] Contains SOURCE/CHANNEL/DATE/CREATOR header
- [ ] Captures all major topics (not just the first section)
- [ ] Preserves specific numbers and statistics
- [ ] MICRO-DETAILS section has specific technical terms
- [ ] TIMELINE section has chronological info
- [ ] No hallucinated content

## Synthetic Questions
- [ ] 8-10 questions generated
- [ ] Questions cover different aspects of the video
- [ ] Questions are specific not generic

## Entities
- [ ] Named people extracted correctly
- [ ] Organizations identified
- [ ] Products/systems captured
- [ ] Aliases included where relevant

## Overall verdict
- [ ] Qwen quality acceptable (proceed with full batch)
- [ ] Qwen quality poor (stick with Haiku)
