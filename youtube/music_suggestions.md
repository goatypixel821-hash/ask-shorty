# Music Suggestions — Calm Techy Instrumental (No Vocals)

Goal: low-key, modern, “developer desk” vibe. 90–120 BPM, no vocals, light percussion, ambient synths.

Use these as *styles* and search terms on your preferred royalty-free site (YouTube Audio Library, Artlist, Epidemic Sound, etc.).

## Recommended Styles / Tags

- Ambient / Chill Electronica
- Lo-fi tech background
- Corporate ambient tech
- Soft synthwave / downtempo
- Ambient timelapse / coding music

## Example Track Keywords

Search for combinations like:

- "calm tech background" (no vocals)
- "ambient technology corporate"
- "lofi coding beat instrumental"
- "majestic timelapse ambient"
- "abstract corporate tech background"

Keep volume low under the voiceover and avoid strong melodic hooks that compete with speech.
